WHY TUNEWAVE?

TuneWave was made initially as a project by a group students at INSA School of engineering, located in Toulouse, France. The idea is to improve your musical & lyrical knowledge all while having fun and competing against friends & people online. More ideas & more features are to come in the near future so stay tuned!
